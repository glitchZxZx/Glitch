Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · glitch-ozuf.onrender.com

Short version: Glitch does not store your conversations. Everything stays in your browser and is gone when you close the tab.

Who runs this

Glitch is a personal AI chat project run by Krystal. Contact: glitch.l.l.m.ai@gmail.com

What data we collect

We collect as little as possible:

  • Your messages are sent to the Groq API to generate a response. They are not stored on our servers.
  • Images you upload are sent directly to the Groq API for processing and are not saved anywhere.
  • Chat history is stored only in your own browser (localStorage) and never leaves your device.
  • We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking of any kind.

Third party services

Glitch uses the following third party services:

  • Groq — processes your messages to generate AI responses. See Groq's privacy policy at groq.com/privacy-policy
  • Render — hosts the website. See Render's privacy policy at render.com/privacy

Your rights (GDPR)

As a user in the EU/EEA you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Since we don't store any personal data, there is nothing to delete. If you have questions, contact us at glitch.l.l.m.ai@gmail.com.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. The date at the top will always reflect the latest version.
