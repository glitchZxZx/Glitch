Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · glitch-ozuf.onrender.com

Short version: If you're logged in, your chat history is saved to Supabase so you can access it across sessions. If you're not logged in, nothing is stored anywhere.

Who runs this

Glitch is a personal AI chat project run by Krystal. Contact: glitch.l.l.m.ai@gmail.com

What data we collect

We collect as little as possible:

  • Your messages are sent to the Groq API to generate a response.
  • Images you upload are sent to the Groq API for processing and are not saved on our servers.
  • If you are logged in: your chat history is saved to Supabase, associated with your account. This is what lets your conversations persist across sessions.
  • If you are not logged in: no chat history is stored anywhere — conversations are lost when you close the tab.
  • Your display name and account information are stored in Supabase to identify your account.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking. A single cookie is used to remember whether you've dismissed our policy update notice.
  • We do not use analytics or third-party tracking of any kind.

How we store your data

Chat history and account information are stored in Supabase, a cloud database service. Data is associated with your account. We keep up to 50 conversations per user. You can delete individual chats at any time from the sidebar. To request full deletion of your data, contact us at glitch.l.l.m.ai@gmail.com.

Third party services

Glitch uses the following third party services:

  • Groq — processes your messages to generate AI responses. See Groq's privacy policy at groq.com/privacy-policy
  • Supabase — stores your account information and chat history. See Supabase's privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy
  • Render — hosts the website. See Render's privacy policy at render.com/privacy
  • Tavily — used for web search when Glitch looks up current information. See tavily.com/privacy

Your rights (GDPR)

As a user in the EU/EEA you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. If you are logged in, this includes your display name and saved chat history. To exercise these rights, contact us at glitch.l.l.m.ai@gmail.com.
